An experimental viewer for digitised files in the National Archives of Australia’s RecordSearch database.

Features include:

  • The wall – view all pages at once
  • Persistent links to individual pages
  • Easy printing of a whole file or selected pages

Connectors:

  • Use one of these connectors to jump from RecordSearch to the Archives Viewer
